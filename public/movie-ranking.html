Sushmith's Favourite Movies

My favourite movies of all time:

Three Idiots

I love this film because it poignantly described my feelings about the Indian education system in which I grew up. It even inspired a short-film I made years later.

The Ramayana

The Japanese produced Ramayana film was one I watched every year as a child. Not only does it depict one of my favourite stories beautifully, but it also features melodic original Sanskrit compositions that serenaded my ears and left me brimming with joy!

RRR

While this is the film that is most likely to be removed from this list, it was definitely the best movie I watched in 2022. Breathtaking action, stunning visuals, great bromance, and inspirational courage.
